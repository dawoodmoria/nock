How to Get First Client — Step by Step
1
Warm network first. Message every person you know. "I'm offering freelance data/AI services. Know anyone who needs help?" 1 in 20 will have a lead.
2
Price to win, not to earn. First 2 clients: charge 50% of your normal rate. Get the testimonial. Then raise prices.
3
Niche down your pitch. Don't say "I do data science". Say "I help e-commerce stores predict inventory demand using ML". Specific wins.
4
Follow up 3 times. 80% of deals close after 3rd follow-up. Most freelancers give up after 1. Don't.
5
Over-deliver to get referrals. Ask for referral within 24hr of project completion: "Who else do you know that might need this?"
